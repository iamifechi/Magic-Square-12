Magic Square - 12!

12 because sum of all rows and columns should be 12. You get the point. See Instructions here.

Instructions

    Here are the conditions:
  • Fill all the boxes (Rows & Columns) with only numbers from 0-8.
  • All boxes must be filled with a number.
  • Do not repeat any number!
  • The sum of each row & each column must be equal to 12.

Don't forget to have fun!!!
